Learn About African Countries and Their Musical Traditions







Drag each country name to where you believe it belongs on the map to the left, then let go of the cursor. An alert will appear to let you know if your were correct of incorrect. If your placement was wrong, the light red highlight will disappear and you may continue to move the country name. If your placement is correct, the highlight will turn to a darker red and the country name will remain fixed in position. If you accidently click on a country, just click again and its color will go back to white. See if you can correctly place the 49 African countries! Double click on any country and a tooltip will appear to give you some options to learn more about the music and the country!
